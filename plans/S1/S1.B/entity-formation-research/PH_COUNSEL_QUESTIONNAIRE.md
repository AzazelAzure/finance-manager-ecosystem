# PH Legal Counsel Consultation — Pre-Engagement Questionnaire

**Purpose:** This document outlines the specific questions and scenarios we need to discuss with our Philippine legal counsel to secure the locked entity pipeline without running afoul of the Anti-Dummy Law or corporate regulations.

## 1. Corporate Governance & Anti-Dummy Law

**Context:** The primary operating entity in the Philippines will be 100% Filipino-owned (spouse-led). HitM (a US citizen) will operate a separate US LLC acting as a technical vendor.

*   **Q1.1: Permissible Roles:** As a foreign spouse, what specific roles am I legally permitted to hold in the PH operating entity? Can I be an authorized bank signatory, or does this trigger Anti-Dummy presumptions?
*   **Q1.2: Management Scope:** What level of "advice" or day-to-day management can I provide to my spouse before it is construed as unauthorized control?
*   **Q1.3: PSP Dashboards:** Does having administrative access to the Payment Service Provider (PSP) dashboards (e.g., PayMongo, Xendit) for API integration purposes violate Anti-Dummy provisions if settlement goes strictly to the spouse-led entity's bank account?

## 2. Entity Choice: DTI Sole Proprietorship vs. OPC

**Context:** We have prepared a preliminary pros/cons comparison (`DTI_VS_OPC_COMPARISON.md`), noting that DTI is simpler but OPC offers limited liability.

*   **Q2.1: Liability Shield:** In the context of a SaaS application, is the liability shield of an OPC necessary to protect our conjugal assets, or is a DTI Sole Proprietorship sufficient given the lower risk profile?
*   **Q2.2: Anti-Dummy Veil:** Does the formal corporate structure of an OPC offer a stronger defense against Anti-Dummy scrutiny compared to a DTI Sole Proprietorship?
*   **Q2.3: BMBE Registration:** Is one structure historically favored by LGUs and the BIR when applying for the Barangay Micro Business Enterprise (BMBE) Certificate of Authority?

## 3. Intercompany IP and Linking Agreements

**Context:** The US LLC will hold the intellectual property (codebase) and provide hosting/technical services to the PH Entity.

*   **Q3.1: Licensing Structure:** How should the software license agreement be structured so that the US LLC is definitively an independent vendor and not an undisclosed principal of the PH Entity?
*   **Q3.2: Arm's-Length Requirements:** What documentation is required by the BIR to prove that the royalty/service fees paid by the PH Entity to the US LLC are at arm's-length?
*   **Q3.3: B2B Payment Flow:** Are there specific withholding tax obligations (e.g., Final Withholding Tax) that the PH Entity must deduct when remitting vendor payments to the US LLC?

## 4. Specific Business Classification

*   **Q4.1: BMBE Service Exclusion:** Under RA 9178 Section 3(a), professional services by licensed professionals are excluded from BMBE. Will our software subscription (SaaS) business be safely classified as an eligible service rather than an excluded profession?
