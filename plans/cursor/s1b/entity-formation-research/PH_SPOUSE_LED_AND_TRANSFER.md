# Philippines — spouse-led entity and later transfer

**Purpose:** Capture **research questions** for the fallback where HitM **cannot** own or control a PH business yet, but the **spouse may** incorporate or register as sole proprietor (or other vehicle) consistent with law. **Not legal advice.**

## Scenario

- Spouse is **eligible** to register a business in the Philippines (citizenship/residency as applicable).
- HitM contributes labor, product, capital, or IP **without** an unlawful circumvention of foreign ownership rules (structure must be vetted — no “straw man” advice here).
- Later, when HitM’s status allows, **ownership or economic interest transfers** or **new share issuance** aligns cap table with intent.

## Questions for PH corporate counsel (checklist)

1. **Vehicle:** Sole proprietorship (DTI) vs **One Person Corporation (OPC)** vs multi-stockholder corp — which matches foreign-equity timeline and payment KYB?
2. **Foreign participation:** At what point can a **non-Philippine national** become stockholder, director, or president under the chosen vehicle?
3. **Transfer mechanics:** Share transfer vs merger vs new parent holding company — tax (CGT, DST, VAT) and **SEC documentary** requirements.
4. **Matrimonial property:** How does **conjugal partnership** or **absolute community** (as applicable) affect valuation and consent for transfers?
5. **Governance during interim:** Employment contract, IP assignment, license from spouse entity to product — **written** boundaries to reduce dispute and clarify who signs for the app in stores and contracts.

## Risk register (conceptual)


| Risk                                                | Mitigation direction                     |
| --------------------------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------- |
| Sham nominee structure                              | Counsel-approved structure only          |
| IP owned by wrong entity                            | Early written assignment or license      |
| PSP KYB mismatch (beneficial owner ≠ account owner) | Disclose true BO per PSP rules           |
| Transfer tax shock later                            | Model transfer now vs later with advisor |
