# Spouse involvement and Anti-Dummy Law — counsel scaffold

**Purpose:** Outline **discussion points** for Philippine counsel: what spouse-led ownership of the PH operating entity tends to require in practice, and how a **separate** US vendor entity might contract with that entity **without** constituting unlawful dummy arrangements. **Not legal advice.**

**Source hygiene:** This file may incorporate **external research drafts**; assertions must be **confirmed** with qualified PH counsel and current SEC/BIR/LGU practice before any structure is relied on commercially.

## 1. Legal context (high level)

**Anti-Dummy Law** (Commonwealth Act No. 108 — use consolidated text and current case practice with counsel) addresses arrangements where **Filipino citizenship** or **minimum Filipino participation** is required by law but a foreigner **evades** that requirement through nominees or concealed control.

**Foreign investment rules:** Minimum capital, reserved activities, and foreign-equity caps depend on the **Foreign Investments Act** (e.g. [RA 7042](https://lawphil.net/statutes/repacts/ra1991/ra_7042_1991.html) as amended) and the **Foreign Investment Negative List**, matched to your **actual business activity** (SaaS subscription vs retail vs other). Do **not** treat informal “USD 200k” summaries as a universal bar without a **written** classification memo.

## 2. Topics counsel typically stress (non-exhaustive)

These are **questions**, not instructions:

- Whether HitM may hold **officer** roles, **signing authority**, or **management contracts** that effectively control a **100% Filipino-owned** enterprise in your sector.
- How **PSP KYB** (beneficial owner, dashboard access, settlement account) must align with **declared** control and ownership.
- How **IP** (codebase, trademarks) should be held and licensed so ownership is clear and transfer pricing is defensible.

## 3. Possible B2B pattern (hypothesis only)

One **pattern** teams explore (viability = **counsel only**):

### Entity A — PH operating company (Filipino-owned vehicle)

- Registered owner is the **eligible spouse** (or other qualified Filipino structure counsel approves).
- **PSP and PH bank** accounts align with DTI/SEC registration and KYB rules.

### Entity B — US technical vendor (HitM-owned LLC or other US vehicle)

- Holds or licenses **IP** and provides **development / hosting** services under arm’s-length terms.

### Linking agreement

- A **written** software license and/or services agreement between A and B, with scope, pricing, and **no** implied management authority over A’s day-to-day business **except as counsel drafts**.

**Royalty / fee split:** Any percentage (e.g. license fee vs local margin) must be **arm’s-length** and documented; **do not** adopt informal “80–90%” figures from chat as tax planning.

## 4. US / PH personal tax

Cross-border flows affect **both** jurisdictions. FEIE and treaty positions are **individual** facts—model only with a **US tax advisor** who has the full picture.

Please review Anti-Dummy Law risk, recommended governance (officers, bank signers, contracts), and a defensible intercompany pricing approach for a subscription SaaS.
