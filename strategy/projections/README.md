## How they relate

- **Quarterly review** looks back at what happened and re-grounds assumptions.
- **Success projection** looks forward using those re-grounded assumptions.
- A quarterly review should trigger a projection refresh, and vice-versa. Keep them in sync;
  when one moves, note the cross-impact in the other.

Canonical multi-year roadmap remains `strategy/strategic-roadmap-reframe-53be/` — projections are
the financial/outcome lens on that roadmap, not a competing plan.
